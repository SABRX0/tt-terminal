Every rug you held was a short you could not take.

Paste any Solana wallet. I will pull every token in it that went to zero, and what it cost. No wallet connect, no signature, nothing to approve. It is read only.

Add a Helius key for real dollar amounts (optional)
Without a key I can only read what is still sitting in the wallet, so you get the list of dead tokens but no cost. With a key I read the full swap history and compute what was actually paid. Free tier at helius.dev. The key stays in this browser tab and is never written into this page.
Numbers are reconstructed from onchain swaps and are approximate. Cost basis ignores tokens received by transfer or airdrop, and skips token to token swaps with no SOL or stablecoin leg. Dead means under $1,000 of liquidity left, which is to say you cannot get out. Good enough to know what the trenches cost you. Not good enough for your accountant.
Holdings and token data from Jupiter. SOL history from Binance. Swap history from Helius. Nothing here is financial advice.
