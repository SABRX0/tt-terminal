Search stays, but it is not the point

The minority who arrive knowing what they want can paste a mint. Everyone else is here to be told what is tradable. That is the opposite of a majors exchange.

🔥 Running hot: short the pump

The strongest angle, and the soul of the product. A token up 240% gets a one tap Short. No other app frames discovery around shorting the hype, because no other app lets you short the long tail. This is the accountability thesis as a home screen row.

🆕 Just made shortable

Our unique freshness hook. "You could not short this yesterday." Every new market we curate is a reason to open the app. Shows both Long and Short since direction is still open.

📉 Bleeding: bounce plays

The other side of the trade, so the feed is not only bearish. Big losers framed as long or bounce candidates.

⭐ Our picks: curation as a feature

Because we choose which tokens get a market, we can have an opinion. Incumbents list everything and drown the user. A short, trusted shortlist is something only a curated venue can offer.

Every row is one tap to a trade

Token, price, open interest, a safety dot, and Long or Short right in the row. Discovery flows straight into the trade sheet. The bottom tab is Discover, not Markets, because finding the trade is the job.

Why this matters: the incumbents can copy our login in a weekend, but they cannot copy a curated, opinionated discovery feed for tokens they structurally cannot list. Discovery plus our AMM-TWAP mechanism is the pairing that is hard to clone. This screen is where the "what can I even trade" problem gets solved, and where the short the pump thesis becomes a habit.
